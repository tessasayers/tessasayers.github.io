San Francisco Giants

The San Francisco Giants were also in on the Bryce Harper sweepstakes. But, the super star didn't end up on the West Coast. While the Giants did offer him a life time contract, it just wasn't enough.

Contract

Most people would think you would be crazy to turn down an opportunity to get paid millions of dollars to play baseball in San Francisco. In that case, I guess would could say Bryce Harper is crazy. Here is what he is rumored to have turned down.

  • 12 years
  • $310 million
Because of California taxes, the Giants would have had to shell out over $335 million to come close to the Phillies offer of $330 million. There were no rumors as to whether the Giants deal inclused a no trade claus or opt outs.
